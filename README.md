## Cash Discount vs. Surcharging

We use a **cash discount program**, not surcharging. The difference matters:

- **Cash discount**: Your posted price is the standard price. Cash customers receive a discount. Legal in all 50 states, supported by all card networks.
- **Surcharging**: A fee added on top of the listed price for card users. Restricted in some states and viewed negatively by customers.

Our program is fully compliant with Visa, Mastercard, and FTC guidelines.
